About Me

I am a former teacher and community organizer who moved from Florida to the Bay Area to pursue a career in software engineering. My work as a community organizer revealed to me that 1)many local non-profit organizations lack the technical resources to maximize the investments of their employees and volunteers 2)most of the popular project management and communication tools do not meet the unique needs of social justice organizations.

In October of 2016 I began working on an app idea centered on accountability for social justice organizations. I then began to teach myself programming through online tutorials. In June of 2017 I left my job as a teacher and since then I have been learning software development full-time.

I am looking to expand my skills at a company that values my non-traditional background, my passion for social justice, and my strive for excellence.
